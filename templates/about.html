Token Live Index is an independent cryptocurrency data aggregation and technical analysis platform. We process raw market data from multiple sources and present it through a unified, zero-latency interface.

What We Do

Multi-Source Data Aggregation

We don't rely on a single API provider. Our backend automatically rotates between three independent data sources to maximize uptime and ensure data accuracy. If one source experiences downtime, the system seamlessly fails over to the next.

Technical Methodology

Pivot Points — Classic formula calculated from daily High, Low, and Close values via Binance kline data.

Sentiment Index — Fear & Greed Index aggregated from market volatility, social media volume, survey data, and BTC dominance.

Fiat Conversion — Real-time USD cross-rates sourced from European Central Bank reference rates.

AI-Assisted Analysis

Human-in-the-Loop Editorial

Our AI Prediction section uses a human-in-the-loop workflow. AI tools assist in drafting initial market analysis, but every piece of content is reviewed, edited, and approved by a human editor before publication. We do not use AI to generate automated trading signals.
