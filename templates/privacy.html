Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Minimal Data Principle: Token Live Index does not require user registration, login, or personal identity verification. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information. Our platform functions entirely as a public data visualization tool.

Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our platform, standard web server logs may temporarily record your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and the time of access. This data is used solely for system security, DDoS protection, and server performance optimization. We do not sell, share, or use this data for marketing purposes.

Local Storage Data

To improve your experience, we use browser local storage to remember your language preference (English/Indonesian) and any analysis drafts you create in the AI Prediction section. This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties. Clearing your browser data will permanently delete this information.

Analytics/Ads: If we integrate third-party analytics or advertising services (such as Google Analytics or Google AdSense) in the future, these services may use cookies to serve relevant content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Cookies

Token Live Index does not use first-party tracking cookies. We do not use cookies for advertising, user profiling, or retargeting. If third-party services like AdSense are active on the page, they may set their own cookies subject to their respective policies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting the core functionality of this platform.

Data Security

We implement standard industry practices to protect against unauthorized access. Our backend API requests are stateless, and we do not store user financial data, private keys, or wallet addresses. However, no method of electronic transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to maintaining a safe browsing environment.
